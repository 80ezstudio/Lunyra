Last updated: 24 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 80EZ Studio handles information when you use our apps. This policy applies to all apps built and published by 80EZ Studio, including Lunyra.

Information We Collect

80EZ Studio does not collect, store, or share personal data from users through our apps.

We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or submit personal information to use our apps.

We do not collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, messages, photos, or payment details through our apps.

How We Use Information

Because 80EZ Studio does not collect personal data through our apps, we do not use personal information for analytics, advertising, profiling, or marketing.

Any settings or preferences you choose within an app are intended only to support that app's features on your device.

Third-Party Services

80EZ Studio does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services to collect personal data from users through our apps.

If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those services are used.

Data Retention

Since 80EZ Studio does not collect or store personal data through our apps, we do not retain personal information on our servers.

Your Choices

You can stop using any 80EZ Studio app at any time by uninstalling it from your device.

Because we do not collect personal data through our apps, there is no user account data for you to access, correct, or delete.

Children's Privacy

80EZ Studio apps are general audience apps and are not directed to children under the age of 13 unless clearly stated otherwise in a specific app listing.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our apps.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
