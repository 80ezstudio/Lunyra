Thoughtfully built

Ambient visual themes

Choose from multiple atmospheric visuals — from a glowing ambient ring that breathes with your session, to a starry night sky that plays quietly in the background. Designed for the dark, and easy on the eyes.

Sound sessions with background play

Run a soundscape session while reading, working, or falling asleep. Audio continues in the background and with the screen off, so you are not locked into keeping the app open.

Guided breathing rhythm

A gentle visual breathing guide helps you find a calm rhythm — nothing intrusive, no voice, no instructions. Just a soft expanding and contracting ring that syncs with a soothing audio bed.

Session timer

Set a session length and let Lunyra run — for focus sessions, a 45-minute wind-down, or until you fall asleep. The session ends gently; your audio fades cleanly.

Privacy and trust

Light on your device
